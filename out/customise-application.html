What You Can Customise

Choose from our flexible library of proven modules — then combine them into one unified application that supports your entire organisation. Each module can be customised to match your business terminology, workflows, and rules.

CRM (Customer Relationship Management)

Manage leads, pipeline, communication, sales analytics, follow-ups, and customer relationships from one place.

Ticketing & Support System

Create, track, and resolve support tickets — internal or customer-facing — with SLA tracking, reporting, and automation.

Project Management

Plan tasks, assign resources, set milestones, and track progress with agile workflows tailored to your team.

Attendance & Time Tracking

Capture accurate check-ins, timesheets, shifts, geofencing, and reporting for payroll or compliance.

HR & Back Office Tools

Custom Dashboards & Reports

Build dashboards that show what you care about — KPI, productivity stats, employee insights, customer growth.
